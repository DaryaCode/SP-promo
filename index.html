FAQ

Common questions

How does SubPar work?

The host books the tee time with the course first, then adds the game in SubPar. SubPar helps verify the details, invite or approve players, message the group, and wrap up the round with ratings and shared moments afterward.

Can courses or pros contact SubPar?

Yes. Golf courses, coaches, caddies, and potential partners can contact the team to learn more about future opportunities.

When will SubPar launch?

SubPar is currently in development. Launch timing, early access, and app store updates will be shared with the waitlist first.

Can I get early access?

Yes. Join the waitlist to be considered for testing and to hear when new beta spots become available.

Will it be on iOS and Android?

The product is being designed mobile-first. Official App Store and Google Play links will be added when releases are ready.

What locations will be supported?

SubPar will roll out by supported areas and courses over time. Join the waitlist to receive availability updates for your area.
